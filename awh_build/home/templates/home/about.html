Who we are

Anderson Wilde & Harris is a Chartered Surveying Practice founded in 1995. We are a multidisciplinary company with a renowned Rights of Light and Party Wall department established for over 20 years. Our main office is located in Holborn, but we assist with projects both in London and nationally. We have an experienced team who have a wealth of knowledge and experience that enables us to work on projects of all sizes. Our expertise, in this unique area of Land and Property is crucial in this sensitive matter and we are able to assist from conception to completion.

Here at Anderson Wilde & Harris we believe in communication, both with our clients and internally. The department works together to guarantee our clients receive a clear and detailed understanding throughout. As experts in property we are considered to be innovative and strategic in our thought process and execution. We are always more than happy to talk through your projects.
