Daylighting and Sunlighting

Daylight and Sunlight Assessments are a common requirement of planning application when reviewing the impact, a proposed development will to surrounding properties light. Review of Daylighting and Sunlight levels can be required to a development and/or to the surrounding properties.

Our Experience

We can provide advice from the beginning stage of a project through a Pre-application and finally onto a Planning Application. The report will assess the properties and the development using the methodologies in the BRE Guidance Site Layout Planning for Daylight and Sunlight. A Guide to Good Practice. Further to this our expertise have provide design advice and we have attended Planning Committee Meetings.

Developer

The complication arising through Rights of Light can be significant. As a Developer you are a risk from time and monetary costs that are unlikely to be recouped. Considering Rights of Light at the acquisition stage can benefit you project by negotiating the purchase price or by providing a contingency if such problems arise.

Neighbour

As the owner of a Rights of Light easement you have certain Rights that entitle you to a remedy. Rights of Light is a very specialist subject and dealing with the Developers and their consultants can be confusing, it is best to considering having your own Rights of Light specialist who can look after your best interests.
